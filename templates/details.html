2. Density Calculation Logic

Unlike traditional timer-based systems, NeuroFlow calculates the "Green Signal Time" dynamically based on the total vehicle density in the frame.

The Formula:

The system calculates a Density Score (DS) by summing the count of vehicles multiplied by their specific weight factor.
